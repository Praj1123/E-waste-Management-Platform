Why Eco-Recycle

E-wastes are electrical and electronic waste. These includes computers, TV appliances, laptops, mobile phones, smartphones, washing machine, fridges electrical toys, Kettler and etc.

  • 01 Creating a safe environment for the comming generation

    E waste are found both in offices and household. Research show that E waste is growing exponentially because global consumer demand continues to increase. Moreover, Technology uptake and shorter replacement cycles are contributing to the growth of E waste.

  • 02 Recycling of the devices

    The global E waste monitor in 2017 show that E waste has grown to 44.7 million metric tons annually. But only 20% of e waste generated were documented to be collected and recycled leaving behind 76% you can do the math. The missing percent is likely to be dumped or recycled under inferior condition.

  • 03 Reusing devices and circuit boards for alternative use
